About the program

Turning creativity into sustainable income.

The program equips participants with digital content creation, influencer marketing, social media management, and digital entrepreneurship skills so they can build careers across YouTube, Instagram, freelance marketing, and campus activation networks.

Why this matters

India’s digital economy and creator ecosystem are expanding quickly, but many aspiring creators still lack platform knowledge, monetization skills, market access, and mentorship.

  • Growing demand in content creation and influencer marketing
  • New income pathways through digital platforms and campaigns
  • Untapped creative talent among youth and women

What do participants become

  • Digital content creators
  • Social media influencers
  • Digital marketing freelancers
  • Campus marketing coordinators

The model combines skill development with hands-on field experience allowing participants to become part of the growing "Orange Economy".

Target Beneficiaries

Focused on accessible opportunity.

Eligibility criteria

  • Age between 18 and 35
  • Resident of the municipal region
  • Access to a smartphone
  • Basic understanding of Social Media platforms like You Tube and Meta.

Priority groups

  • Women seeking opportunities to generate income or additional income.
  • Youth from economically weaker sections
  • Individuals with creative skills
  • Aspiring digital entrepreneurs

Training Structure

Three months of blended learning.

The program combines online digital skills development in months 1 and 2 with offline practical implementation and campus network building in month 3.

Month 1

Online training

Participants build foundational technical, creative, and platform skills for digital careers.

Month 2

Online training

Participants strengthen content, marketing, and monetization skills through guided assignments.

Month 3

Practical field training

Participants apply learning through real campus engagement and local brand promotion.
